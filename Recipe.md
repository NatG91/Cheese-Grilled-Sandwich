Recipe

Cheese grilled sandwich

Cooking Time: 30 mins
Serves 4 people

1. In a hot girdle pan add oil 
2. Add finely chopped onions and saute till golden brown
3. Add finely chopped green capsicum and saute till golden brown
4. Add black pepper powder and slices of cheddar chees 
5. Add some salt to taste
6. Add ketchup 
7. Light brown bread slices in a toaster 
8. Butter the toasted bread slices
9. Fill up the slices with filling ,of onion and capsicum and make a sandwich
10. Grill the sandwiches till golden brown and serve with tomato ketchup
